Context: [context/viewer.md](../context/viewer.md).

The reference's voice-morphing floater (paid Vivox "voice fonts" that
disguise the speaker's voice). **Won't do**: voice morphing is a
Vivox-service feature, and SL's voice stack has moved to WebRTC
([[viewer-voice-audio]] targets that), where no equivalent service
exists — the feature is effectively sunset upstream. Recorded during the
voice scope revision (voice audio itself is now in scope) so the gap is a
decision, not an oversight.

Reference (Firestorm, read-only): `llfloatervoiceeffect` /
`floater_voice_effect.xml`.
